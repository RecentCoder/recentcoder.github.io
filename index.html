Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the community?
Simply visit our Telegram group @RecentCoders and click "Join". Make sure to read and agree to our community guidelines before participating.

Contact @RecentCoder on Telegram with proof of the violation. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

Post your questions in our Telegram group. Remember to include relevant code snippets and what you've tried so far. Our community is always ready to help!

Share your knowledge, answer questions, participate in discussions, and help maintain a positive environment. You can also contribute to our open-source projects on GitHub.
